We Need Your Help

To be able to compete in the Botball Competition

The registration fee for the Los Angeles competition is $2200. Costs increase if we qualify for further rounds of the competition.

By Donating

You Get

Your company's logo on our team t-shirts, and on our website. The logo on our website will link to your website and has a customizable line of text underneath, or you can choose to just use the name of your company.

We Get

Funding for our team. Since this is a student-run club, we need funding for transportaion, competition fees, website hosting fees, and even for food during meetings and competitions. Anything you can give goes a long way.
